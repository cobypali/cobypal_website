About Coby

Los Angeles-based filmmaker, creative, and producer.

Coby is the founder and Executive Producer at Kippy Studios and also works as a Creative Producer for Meta. In his free time, he enjoys writing about media, philosophy, and other topics that you can read via Coby's Brain. He also likes adventures with friends and family, health and fitness, and learning new things. He has a BA from Stanford in Philosophy with a minor in Film and Media Studies and an MFA in Animation and Digital Arts from USC's School of Cinematic Arts.
